London Bridge, England
Kensington Palace, England

England is a part of the United Kingdom, bordering the countries of Wales and Scotland. I visited England for the first time in 2018 for about 10 days and spent most of my time in London. London s a beautiful city filled with exciting nightlife an the most beautiful downtown streets. When in London, make sure you go to a true english pub and eat some Nandos! During my time in London, I visited numerous museums, shops and sights. I visited famous landmarks like the London Bridge, Buckingham and Kensignton Palace and the London Eye. My advice is to not waste your money on the London Eye. The view is not that great and the ticket is pretty expensive. One of my favourite parts of my trip was seeing the live broadway version of Mamma Mia. I love Abba, so watching this play in a beautiful old school theatre was so worth it. London is an amazing city and I would suggest that everyone has to see it at least once in their lifetime :)
